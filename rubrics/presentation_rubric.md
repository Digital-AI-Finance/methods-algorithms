# Presentation Rubric

**Course**: Methods and Algorithms - MSc Data Science
**Duration**: 15 minutes per presentation
**Total Points**: 100

---

## Quick Grading Guide

For each criterion, circle the appropriate level. Sum the points for the final grade.

---

## Criterion 1: Content Accuracy (25 points)

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Excellent | 25 | All facts correct, sources properly cited, demonstrates deep understanding |
| Good | 20 | Minor errors (1-2), most sources cited, solid understanding |
| Adequate | 15 | Some errors (3-4), few citations, basic understanding |
| Needs Work | 10 | Major errors (5+), no citations, superficial understanding |

**Score: _____ / 25**

**Notes:**
- [ ] Facts verified
- [ ] Sources cited
- [ ] Demonstrates understanding

---

## Criterion 2: Clarity and Organization (25 points)

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Excellent | 25 | Crystal clear structure, logical flow, smooth transitions |
| Good | 20 | Clear structure, mostly logical, minor jumps |
| Adequate | 15 | Somewhat organized, some unclear sections |
| Needs Work | 10 | Confusing structure, hard to follow |

**Score: _____ / 25**

**Notes:**
- [ ] Clear introduction
- [ ] Logical progression
- [ ] Strong conclusion

---

## Criterion 3: Visual Aids and Slides (25 points)

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Excellent | 25 | Professional design, visuals enhance understanding, readable |
| Good | 20 | Good design, helpful visuals, mostly readable |
| Adequate | 15 | Basic design, visuals present but not always helpful |
| Needs Work | 10 | Poor design, distracting or no visuals |

**Score: _____ / 25**

**Notes:**
- [ ] Readable fonts/colors
- [ ] Appropriate charts/diagrams
- [ ] Not too much text per slide

---

## Criterion 4: Timing and Delivery (25 points)

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Excellent | 25 | 14-16 minutes, well-paced, confident delivery |
| Good | 20 | 12-18 minutes, mostly well-paced, good delivery |
| Adequate | 15 | 10-20 minutes, uneven pacing, adequate delivery |
| Needs Work | 10 | <10 or >20 minutes, poor pacing, unclear delivery |

**Score: _____ / 25**

**Notes:**
- [ ] Within time limit
- [ ] Good pacing
- [ ] Clear speech
